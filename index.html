Theory × Implementation × Design × Human

  In my opinion, a student majoring Computer Science should concern about 4 subjects: Theory, the base of knowledge. You might be good at coding works, but you should never forget those great people and their foundations. If you don't realize theory, you won't invent something so creative that can exceed predecessors. Implementation, well... I mean, "coding" or "programming". You invent it, you do it, and then... show your masterpiece to the world. Design, the art of engineering. How important do you think design and taste are? Steve Jobs (I'm NOT Jobs fan) just proved it. Human, you're not a coding machine or auto paper generator. As long as you're a human being, you should always care about what other people think you are, what they want, what you want, and what kind of person do you wanna be. Sometimes you will find these questions are more complicated than knowledge you've ever learned in the class; and that, is the reason why we live.
